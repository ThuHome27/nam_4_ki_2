1. Introduction
- He tuong tac: he thong nhan dau vao tu user va co the cung cap 
thong tin nhu dau ra cho user.
- Dinh nghia HCI:
 + Baecker Buxton: HCI bao gom tap process, action, dialogues 
ma user su dung de tuong tac voi may tinh nham thuc hien mot task cu the.
 + ACM: thiet ke, danh gia, cai dat cac he tinh toan tuong tac 
cho con nguoi su dung va nghien cuu cac van de xung quanh no.

- HCI maps: User and Context, Human, Computer, Development process
- Development process:
 + Waterfall model: Initiation -> Phan tich -> Thiet ke -> Cai dat
 + Star model: co the bat dau tu bat ki buoc nao, tao ra mau thu nhanh

- Development process can dam bao:
 + dau vao dung, dau ra dung
 + dau vao sai, cho phep nhap lai
 + de dung, than thien

- Tac dung cua HCI:
 + Khoa hoc may tinh, AI, tam li xa hoi, khoa hoc nhan thuc, thiet ke do hoa, 
cong thai hoc, xa hoi hoc.

- Tinh dung duoc:
 + bao gom: de hoc, de su dung, trai nghiem tot
 + phu thuoc thiet ke, cai dat he tuong tac
 + danh gia tren: nang suat, hieu qua, su thoa man.
