b9. Task analysis
- Phan tich nhiem vu: la xac dinh hanh dong the chat hoac nhan thuc cua nguoi dung 
nham thuc hien mot muc tieu biet truoc. 
- Phan tich nhiem vu (dn): phan tich cach nguoi dung thuc hien cac 
nhiem vu de dat duoc muc dich cua ho.
- GOMS: mo hinh phan tich dua tren nhan thuc bao gom ca phan tich vat li va  
suy nghi can thuc hien de hoan thanh muc dich.
- HTA: mo hinh phan tich dua tren hanh dong cho phep phan tach nhiem vu o muc cao thanh cac 
nhiem vu o muc con, thao tac hoac hanh dong.
- mo hinh nhan thuc: tap trung vao qua trinh nhan thuc lien quan den cac hanh dong
- mo hinh hanh dong: tap trung vao cac hanh vi

10.1.3 chuan ISO 13407 cho UCD 
Nguyen tac:
- can su tham gia tich cuc cua user
- phan dinh chuc nang hop li giua nguoi dung va he thong
- cac giai phap thiet ke lap
- doi thiet ke am hieu nhieu linh vuc

Hanh dong:
- hieu va xac dinh boi canh su dung
- xac dinh yeu cau nguoi dung va to chuc
- tao cac giai phap thiet ke san pham (prototype)
- tham dinh thiet ke
(lap lai cac buoc)

Tai sao can UCD:
- cai thien tinh dung duoc va huu dung cua san pham
- tim kiem cau tra loi ve user va cac task, goal cua user 
de tu do giup phat trien va design.

10.2 Prototyping
- Prototyping:
    + Why? : 1. get feedback early
	     2. experiment with alternatives: select best one (thu nghiem nhanh)
	     3. experiment with alternatives: debug, ... (de thay doi)
    + do tin cay: interface, interaction
	- low: tap trung vao interface (khong co interaction)
	- hight: giong voi ban finish (co ca tuong tac va giao dien)
10.2.3
- Thiet ke do tin cay thap: Sketch (phac thao), Storyboarding (kich ban)
(dung but, giay)
- Thiet ke do tin cay cao (hi-fi prototyping): 
computer-based simulation, wizard of oz, slide shows and video prototyping
+ computer-based simulation : 
  ' vertical prototypes
  ' horizontal prototypes
  ' scenario
